WHO ARE WE?

University of Yangon Environmental Club (abbreviated YUEC) is University student-run organization, founded by students from the University of Yangon since 2017, administering environmental conservation works and knowledge enhancement in University of Yangon. University of Yangon Environmental Club is divided into 7 teams, each headed by a team leader:

  1. Administration Team
  2. Public Relations Team
  3. Human Resource Team
  4. Project Team
  5. Research Team
  6. Finance Team and
  7. Social Media Team.

YUECs expects to provide YU Community with a clean and green study campus and living environment. With over 3 years of experience, YUEC is strongly supported by the University.
